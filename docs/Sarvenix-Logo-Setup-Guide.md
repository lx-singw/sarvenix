## How to Update the Logo in Slack

1. Go to the [Slack App Directory Developer Dashboard](https://api.slack.com/apps).
2. Select your **Sarvenix** application from the list.
3. In the left-hand navigation bar, click on **Basic Information**.
4. Scroll down to the **App Credentials** and **Display Information** sections.
5. In the **Display Information** section, locate the **App icon** setting.
6. Click **Add App Icon** (or **Change Icon**) and upload the premium logo file:
   📁 `docs/assets/sarvenix-project-logo.png`
7. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the page.

The new logo will immediately propagate to your Slack workspace, replacing the generic default bot icon!
